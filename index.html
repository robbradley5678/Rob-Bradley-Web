Brighter Homes Ltd

Redesign / Brochure Site

A ground-up redesign for an established building contractor. Modernising their online presence to properly reflect a 35-person firm with nearly 60 years of history.

HTML CSS JavaScript
Learn more Visit site

Brighter Homes are an established building contractor with around 35 employees, founded in 1966, specialising in high-value restoration and renovation projects. Their existing site, built years earlier from a template, no longer reflected the scale or reputation of the business. They needed something that felt considered and professional, and that properly showcased the quality of their work.

The brief centred on three things: modernise the look and feel, tell the company's story, and build a portfolio section that could do justice to their projects. Each portfolio entry has its own page with an overview, a detailed breakdown of the work, and before-and-after photography.

The result was a ground-up redesign and rebuild - clean, structured, and built to perform. Lighthouse scores sit at 90+ across the board. The managing director's verdict on seeing the finished site: "I bloody love it."

ECUMaster Ireland

Ecommerce Build / Personal Project

A full ecommerce solution for the official Irish distributor of ECUMaster engine management products: taking them from phone orders to a self-sufficient online store with 400+ SKUs.

WordPress WooCommerce Flatsome Theme
Learn more Visit site

ECUMaster Ireland is the official Irish distributor of ECUMaster performance engine management products. The client, a contact from my previous career in the automotive industry, had an outdated WordPress site that hadn't been maintained in years. Orders were being handled entirely by phone and in person. He needed a proper ecommerce solution that could do the selling for him.

Given complete creative freedom, I researched popular ecommerce sites in the automotive aftermarket world, and drew on the best of what I found to shape the design and layout - something that felt modern and credible for a technical, enthusiast audience.

The product catalogue presented a practical challenge: ECUMaster don't provide a ready-to-import CSV for WooCommerce. Rather than entering 400+ products manually, I used AI tooling to help scrape their dealer portal and generate a structured import file - pulling SKUs, pricing, descriptions and images in one pass. The site also includes documentation and product guides to reduce customer support queries.

The result is a clean, self-sufficient operation. Orders come in, he ships them out.

MTS Customz are a vehicle upholstery and customisation specialist with a strong reputation and a loyal social following, but no website to bring it all together. Their client list ranges from everyday repairs to high-end full retrims for footballers arriving in brand new Porsches. They needed a site that matched that energy.

The brief was simple: make it look cool. With a library of professional photography at my disposal, I let the work speak for itself - building around it with a dark theme, parallax backgrounds, and individual service pages covering bespoke interiors, coloured seat belts, van conversions, audio installations and detailing.
